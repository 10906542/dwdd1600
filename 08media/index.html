I'm Seth Parry, a graphic designer working for and studying at UVU.

This is a small selection of my work, highlighting different projects and design approaches I've explored.

I enjoy working across different mediums, from graphic design to web development, constantly finding ways to challenge myself creatively. This collection reflects my ability to adapt to diverse styles and project goals, while maintaining a strong focus on detail and functionality. Each piece has been an opportunity to experiment and learn, helping me grow as a designer.

Each piece represents my process of experimenting with ideas and refining them into designs. I’m always exploring new ways to create and grow as a designer.

Design is about more than aesthetics; it’s about problem-solving and storytelling. My process often begins with curiosity and experimentation, followed by refining ideas into purposeful, impactful designs. I’m always seeking inspiration, whether through collaboration, research, or trying something entirely new. This collection is a glimpse into my ongoing journey as a designer.
